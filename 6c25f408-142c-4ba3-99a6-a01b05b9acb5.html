Pyramidal Break's website

Pyramidal Break™ is all about sumo battles between warriors from ancient civilizations! Press the prompted button to keep pushing your opponent forward, until someone throws the other out of the platform!





Currently, the demo only supports local multiplayer. This means, two people can battle to push each other out of the stage! A single player campaign is planned for the Play Store release.
