Eves Drop is a fast-paced, physics-based action game about free-falling through cyberspace inspired by the extreme hackers of the 1990s.

  • Ricochet enemies for in-your-face destruction
  • Fluid, acrobatic gameplay about falling...with style!
  • Gritty, sci-fi, pixel art by Kyle Templeton (@supajackle)
  • Original, old-school, techno soundtrack by Rupert Cole (@RupertCole)
  • Bite-sized 5 minute runs
  • Real moody and Cool


New Year's Eve, 1999.

You are the head of an elite underground hacking ring, Project EVE.

Your goal: Stopping the world's largest surveillance corporation, Big Brother, from turning into a deep-state puppet master.

You've spent months setting up this operation.

Tonight, at midnight, you'll have your chance.

Crash their code

Breach their protocols
