Descripción

Gumgem 2.0 is here!

Now available for GameShell too!

Get into a procedurally generated castle!

The gummy kingdom is in danger! The evil gummy bear has taken the prince prisoner!




Like never before we need a hero who knows how to get the prince out of trouble!


Oh, that's you...!? 


You're our only hope! Please, go rescue the prince!
Oh, a while ago we renovated the castle, you may not recognize it from the last time.

Good Luck!


To play Gumgem, a controller is highly recommended.


Features v2.0:

  • Venture into the a procedurally generated castle, different every time you play!
  • Several types of castle rooms, each with its win conditions.
  • Cute and colorful pixel art.
  • Tight controls and challenging platforming!
  • Enemies and gummy bears to defeat.
  • Unique take on dash-based platformers.
  • Defeat the Evil Gummy Bear and rescue the prince!
  • Witty retro-style soundtrack!

Notes on v1.0:

  1. THIS IS A VERY OUTDATED VERSION OF THE GAME, LACKS MANY FEATURES AND IS NOT INDICATIVE OF THE QUALITY OF LATER VERSIONS.
  2. The core mechanics, controls and general feel of the game are very different compared to v2.0.
  3. The game is exported with debug active due to issues with the alpha version of the engine. Fixed in v2.0.
  4. There are known and sporadic bugs, some of which are engine bugs.
    Most of them are fixed in v2.0.
