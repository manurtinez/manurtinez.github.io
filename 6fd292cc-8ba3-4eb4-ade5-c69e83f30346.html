Descripción

Nuvoloso is a casual arcade game where your task is to look at a sky full of clouds and find clouds in a certain shape. 

Singleplayer

Play through a number of singleplayer game modes and try to improve your highscore while also unlocking new backgrounds & powers that will give you an advantage.

Classic - the original game where you have all the time in the world to find as many clouds as you can. Don't make too many mistakes though, because 3 strikes and it's game over.

Tick Tock - the timed game mode where you don't need to worry about strikes or clouds floating away, just the timer that will limit your highscore. 

Color Match - the colorful game mode where all clouds have a random color and you need to worry about their color as well as their shape.

Local Multiplayer

VS - play against a friend in this local multiplayer game mode to see who can find the most clouds the quickest. 

Educational

Nuvoloso is a fun way to memorize or practice some English words for English Language Learners.
