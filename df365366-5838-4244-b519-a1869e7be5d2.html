Info

Requiem Aeternam Deo is the second installment of Hello Charlotte series, released in 2016.

It's a story lamenting the impossibility of universal happiness, and idolization of those out of our reach. As her mind unravels, ink will flow, and blood-red flowers will bloom in the desolate field.

Features

  • A mix of exploration, puzzles, psychological and general knowledge tests
  • Poetic atmosphere
  • Stories inside a story
  • Multiple endings
  • Outdated humor then and there
  • Extra content includes an art pack containing CGs / development art for both EP1 and EP2, and a minimalist music album by etherane
