Just in time for Halloween! Build your own cute Zombie and possibly survive the apocalypse or at least just have a new cute icon!

Music and Sound Effects by 'Moode Myoozik (https://moodemyoozik.ca/)

  • 115 different customization options
  • Save Screenshot option 
  • and more... 

Drag and drop the  items and position them

Middle Click (on the mouse) moves an item down in the hierarchy
