INDECT
For the Insecurity of Citizens

 

THIS IS IN VERY EARLY TESTING

 

due to technical difficulties further development is on hold until the Godot Engine 4 release

Sorry for having not updated in over years now, but my old codebase on the old engine version just was very exhausting to maintain, and given it's not something i can work on 9-to-5 i decided to just wait with the rewrite. This project is not canceled, just on hiatus for now.

 

 

Current state:

  •  the demo is exactly the same as the full version (so don't buy it yet unless you want to give me free money).
  • there's only a few levels
  • the story isn't fleshed out yet
  • but i've already hidden an easter egg somewhere in the code, let's see if someone finds it 😉

consider this a proof of concept / prototype release, more content will be added.

We have a Discord you can leave any feedback on, would be greatly appreciated.

Alternatively any issues can be reported on our Gitlab or via E-Mail.

Oh and since apparently you need one of those nowadays, the Roadmap is public (joke aside, that's literally just where I organize upcoming features I'm brainstorming about, some might not make it, some might be made that never were on it, don't sue me 😜).


You might have to click the game after it loads for it to recognize keyboard input.

Controls:

  • [Left] - Walk / Climb Left
  • [Right] - Walk / Climb Right
  • [Up] - Climb Up / Walk through level doors
  • [Down] - Climb / Crouch Down
  • [Space] - Jump
  • [Alt] or [X] - Shoot
  • [Escape] - Menu
