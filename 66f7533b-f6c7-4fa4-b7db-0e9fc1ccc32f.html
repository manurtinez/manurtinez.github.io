Descripción

Casual football & strategy videogame.
Real-time simulator that gives you full control of your favorite football club.

What Is It? Simple, really…

You are the BOSS!!!

Football Club Simulator - FCS gives you total control of your team through an retro, elegant and powerful interface.

This means that the gamer gets to make all the decisions of his favorite football club as head coach, manager, and chairman.

In addition, you can try out your decisions in the “Strategic match” while giving orders to your payers in Real-time.

Let your inner football strategist out!!!
You realize in just minutes that your decision are making the difference[/b] and you are leading your team to glory.

And all this in Real-time!!!

Football Club Simulator - FCS is agile, intuitive, fast... and allows you to play seasons in hours, not days: line-up, training, tactics, youth squad, scout, transfers, stadium construction, highlights mode and much, much more...

# 7 national leagues, including first and second divisions of the following countries:
England, Spain, Germany, Italy, France, Mexico and Argentina.
# 37 national and continental competitions from Europe and America.
# 501 teams from 31 countries all around the world.
# 16.873 players from 148 different nationalities.

Do not miss the “Strategic Match”
Experience the game from the "Dug-out"

In the “Strategic match” you’ll see your players doing what you’ve been working at training sessions during the week and playing to your tactics. And this it´s only the first step of your victory.

Most importantly, when the key moment arises, you can give orders to your players in Real-time. They’ll instantly carry out, giving you the chance to lead your team to glory.

When you make the calls in the “Strategic match”, you will see stunning moves and incredible goals.

Enjoy them both as they happen and while watching the replays.

Try the “Plays chalkboard”
Football is not only about winning, it´s about winning in style!!!

We all know exactly how we’d have our teams playing if only we had the chance.
The “Plays chalkboard”, another Football Club Simulator - FCS exclusive, has put this within your reach.

Freeze the match, study the players’ positions and draw in the movements, actions, passes and shots you want them to make.

Use the power of “Command system”
Never forget… You Are the BOSS!!!

The “Command system” offers you a wide range of orders with which to change the flow of the match.

> Defense
Order your players to clear the lines, play the offside trap, up the intensity or keep hold of the ball as part of your strategy.

> Attack
Pick the right moment to order your players to play the ball into the hole, open it up out wide, move it around or go route one.

Feel the emotion of a true coach with “Real-time orders” at match day
Command your team in Real-time!!!

In each half of the Strategic match; you'll have 20 seconds to run each order.
Your technical team has already got this covered.

+40', +60', +80'… Train specifically so that you can add up minutes to carry out each order on match day. Another step towards victory.

Enjoy the “Real-time shot”
Do not hold back! HIT IT!!!

We proudly present the thrilling Real-time command.

When one of your players has a clean sight at goal, do not hold back, give the order "HIT IT!" and he'll shoot. Rise up your chances of score!
